Belly Button Bacteria

Bacteria lives in harmony with the human body, many of which is not harmful, and recent studies have shown that many species live in our belly buttons. With over 2000 species found, and each profile unique, belly button bacteria continues to capture the interest of many researchers. Belly Button Biodiversity investigates the different species of bacteria living in our belly button. The data presented on this webpage is the result of swabbing the belly buttons of volunteers.
